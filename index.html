Jeff Bridges's Acting Abilities

Introduction

The Dude (The Big Lebowski)

Summary

In 1998, Jeff Bridges played "The Dude" in the film "The Big Lebowski". At this time Jeff was already a well-regarded character actor. Even though "The Dude" is supposed to be a representation of a slacker that lives in Los Angeles, as you are watching the film it is impossible to not like the character and find him humorous with Bridges playing this role

Kevin Flynn (Tron and Tron: Legacy)

Summary

Jeff Bridges first played Kevin Flynn in Tron (1982) and then again in Tron: Legacy in 2010. In both films Bridges does a great job of playing a role of hero that ultimately wants to use technology for the greater good of humanity. In both films he is both determined and logical and is considerate of those he cares for.

Obadiah Stane (Iron Man)

Summary

In 2008, Jeff Bridges co-starred with Robert Downey Jr. in the film Iron Man. Bridges plays Obadiah Stane, the second-in-command at Stark Industries and a friend and mentor to Tony Stark who is played by Robert Downey Jr. In the beginning of film Obadiah comes off as a really charming and inovative person. However as the film progresses you learn of his real intentions and realize that he is a villain in disguise.
